 $2^n$ -> $2 \times2 \times2 \times2 ......$   


- Product rule
> $$X^n . X^m = X^{n+m}$$
> 

- Quotient Rule
> $$ \frac {X^m} {X^n} = X^{m-n}   $$
> 

- The power rule
>$$(X^n)^m = X^{n.m}$$ 

- The power of Zero
> $$X^0 = 1$$

- Negative power
> $$X^{-n} = \frac{1}{X^n}$$

- Fraction Exponents
>$$X ^ {\frac{1}{n}} = \sqrt[n]{X}$$

- Distributed Exponent over product
>$$(X.Y)^n = X^n .Y^n $$

- Distributed Exponent over quotient
>$$(\frac{X}{Y})^n = \frac{X^n}{Y^n}$$
